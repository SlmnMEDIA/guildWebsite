About Us

<Acumen> is a recently formed guild located on the Zul'jin server. We consist of several friends who wanted a more relaxed schedule and raid environment while still completing progression in a timely manner. Most of us have done hardcore raiding, achieving top 30 US kills in the past, but now have real lives to attend to. We are looking for like-minded players that want to surround themselves with levelheaded people in order to clear all the content there is to offer while it is still current.

If you think you would be a good fit, we encourage you to apply regardless of our current recruitment needs. All exceptional applicants will be considered so long as you have a relaxed attitude and are enjoyable to raid with. We hope to hear from you soon!

Raiding Information

Our current schedule is:

Tuesday: 8:00 p.m. - 12:00 a.m. (EST)
Wednesday: 8:00 p.m. - 12:00 a.m. (EST)
Thursday: 8:00 p.m. - 12:00 a.m. (EST)

All loot is distributed via loot council, which consists of four officers and one random rotating raider that changes on a weekly basis.

The trial period is only two weeks as we feel that is enough time for us to see if we are a good fit for each other. During the trial period, we expect a minimum of 90% attendance so we can better evaluate you in a raid environment.

If you have any inquiries, contact an officer in-game or leave a post on our forums. We hope to hear from you soon!
